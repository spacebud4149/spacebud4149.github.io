What is Metaverse Monday?

Metaverse Monday is a Twitter space where like-minded and curious individuals come together on a weekly basis every Monday at 5:30 pm EST to 7:30 pm EST.

We discuss the metaverse and its role in web 3 alongside the convergence of AI and blockchain and how it will shape the open world metaverse in the future!

Some notable guests:

  • Rob Grieg
  • Paul Manuel
  • Robert Scoble

How to listen?

  1. Follow Jspeak on Twitter
  2. Tune in live at 5:30 pm EST - 7:30 pm EST
  3. Or listen to the recordings which you can find on Twitter and here!
